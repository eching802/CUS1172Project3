Interests



At Christoforou Labs our studies focus Computational Neuroscience and observing brain wave patterns to develop our tools. We want to evolve humans by encorperating advance Machine Learning technology to everyday life activities and problems. By using the most advance Vision Brain-computer Interfaces we can bring dreams that we can only experince in our minds to life. We can also help solve pathological disorders that affect millions of people everyday. We hope to elminate medication which has to be refilled every month and can become expensive. Essentially Christoforou Labs is trying to create a more economic alternative which can presisly regulate hormones without the worrying of ingesting medication that can have dangerous side affects.
